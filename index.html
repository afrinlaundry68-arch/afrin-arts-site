About

An institution for African theatre and cultural memory

AFRIN Arts is a creative organisation committed to producing theatre and cultural experiences rooted in African heritage, founded and led by Jatto Godswill Osose (Jatto Whisper), a Nigerian theatre director, playwright, producer, and cultural strategist.

The Festival

AFRIVORA: theatre, culture, and trade in a single day

AFRIVORA brings together a stage play, a cultural festival, and a trade fair, with the ambition of becoming an annual fixture every July. The inaugural edition features NEKIGHIDI, staged at the YWCA Hall on 10th July 2026.
