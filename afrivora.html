The Festival

AFRIVORA: theatre, culture, and trade in a single day

AFRIVORA brings together a stage play, a cultural festival, and a trade fair, with the ambition of becoming an annual fixture every July. The inaugural edition features NEKIGHIDI, a stage play written by Ossa Earliece and performed partly in Edo, staged at the YWCA Hall rather than a conventional theatre.
